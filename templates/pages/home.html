tools

here are some tools used in combating crimes

ALPR
Takes live camera and video. Detects vehicle and decodes license plate, vehicle make, model,color and more.
Biometrics
The measurement and statistical analysis of people's unique physical and behavioural characteristics. The technology is mainly used for identification and access control.
ShotSpotter
This is a gunshot detection system. Precision policing solutions that help save lives, deter crimes, and make communities safer.
Police Drone
It comes with a high-performance graphics processor and uses the Nivida Jetson module for ultra-high high-performance and energy efficiency. It can run modern AI workloads.
Tasers
A taser is a non-lethal electroshock weapon used to incapacitate targets via shocks that temporarily impair's the target's function.
Body Camera
Fully-featured high definition video + audio body worn camera designed to collect video,audio, and images of public events That the user is involved in.
